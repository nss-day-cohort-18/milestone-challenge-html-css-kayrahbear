About

Hi, I'm Kayla Brewer! I'm a 26 year old Apprentice Software Developer at Nashville Software School studying front and back end software development. Studies include HTML, CSS, Git/GitHub, Javascript/JQuery, Python, the command line, and frameworks like Bootstrap and Angular.

I have Alopecia, an autoimmune disease that causes hairloss, but I don't let it get me down. I enjoy crafting (especially fiber related crafts. I have my own spinning wheel!), playing video games, and telling my toddler not to touch things. For me, software development is the greatest creative outlet around. With nothing more than a computer, a person can create applications to do literally anything. That's amazing!
